Crystal Collector!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matchong your total score to the random numbeer generated at the start of the game. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number that was generated at the start of the game.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the random numbeer generated will be different and the value of each crystal will be different.
